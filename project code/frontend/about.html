About DúnKey

Built with security, simplicity, and trust at the core.

Who We Are

DúnKey was developed as a third-year project by cybersecurity students Alex, James, and Ganster, with a focus on building practical tools for real-world password protection.

Why We Built DúnKey

Weak and reused passwords are one of the top reasons for account breaches. Our mission is to provide a strong, clean, and secure way for users to manage their credentials.

What Makes It Secure

DúnKey uses AES-256 encryption for storing password vaults, bcrypt for login credentials, and runs vulnerability testing to ensure the highest standard of security.

Future Goals

We plan to expand DúnKey with browser extensions, mobile apps for iOS and Android, and secure syncing across devices.
