Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage my passwords?

Once logged in, Go to your dashboard. From there you can then search, filter by strength, add new entries, or edit/delete existing ones.

What if I forget my master password?

Your vault is encrypted client-side and cannot be recovered without the original password. If you forget it, contact support for assistance.

Can I change the theme and notifications?

Yes—under “Profile” → “App Preferences,” you can toggle dark mode and enable or disable notifications to suit your workflow.

How do I contact support?

Use the “Contact” page to send us a message. You’ll get an automatic acknowledgment, and our team aims to respond within 24 hours.
