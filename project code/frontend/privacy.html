Privacy Policy

Your privacy and security come first at DúnKey.

Introduction

Welcome to DúnKey's Privacy Policy. As a trusted password manager, we prioritize your security and privacy. This policy details how we collect, process, and protect your sensitive information using state-of-the-art security protocols.

Data Collection & Storage

We operate under a zero-knowledge architecture. This means we never store or have access to your actual passwords or private data. All sensitive data is encrypted client-side using AES-256 encryption before it ever reaches our servers.

Data Security & Encryption

Your security is ensured through industry-leading standards including AES-256 encryption, PBKDF2 key derivation, and secure SSL/TLS communication. We also support Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) for enhanced account security.

Breach Protocols

In the unlikely event of a data breach, our protocols ensure immediate containment, user notification, and forensic analysis. Since we do not store plaintext data, any compromised data would remain encrypted and unreadable.

Cookies & Tracking

We use only essential cookies required for secure user sessions. No tracking or advertising cookies are used. You can manage cookie preferences directly through your browser settings.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. Limited data sharing only occurs to comply with legal obligations or when you explicitly authorize it for services like account recovery.

Your Rights & Control

You maintain full control over your data. You may access, export, or permanently delete your data at any time. For assistance, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@dunkey.com.
